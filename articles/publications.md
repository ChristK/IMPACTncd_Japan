# Publications

## Scientific Publications

This page lists scientific publications that describe, validate, or use
the IMPACTncd-Japan model.

### Primary Model Publications

#### Main Model Description

**Kypridemos, C., et al. (2025).** The IMPACTncd-Japan model: A
comprehensive microsimulation model for cardiovascular disease policy
evaluation in Japan. *The Lancet Regional Health - Western Pacific*,
*54*, 101623.

- **DOI**:
  [10.1016/j.lanwpc.2025.101623](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.lanwpc.2025.101623)
- **Description**: This is the primary publication describing the
  IMPACTncd-Japan model, including its structure, validation, and
  initial policy applications.
- **Key Features**: Model architecture, data sources, validation
  results, and example policy scenarios

#### Model Framework and Methodology

**Kypridemos, C., et al. (2022).** Future projections of cardiovascular
disease mortality and the potential impact of policy interventions in
Japan: A modelling study. *The Lancet Regional Health - Western
Pacific*, *29*, 100637.

- **DOI**:
  [10.1016/j.lanwpc.2022.100637](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.lanwpc.2022.100637)
- **Description**: Early work on cardiovascular disease projections in
  Japan that informed the development of the IMPACTncd-Japan model.
- **Key Features**: Baseline projections, policy scenario analysis, and
  methodological foundations

### Citation Guidelines

#### Citing the Model

When using the IMPACTncd-Japan model in your research, please cite the
primary model publication:

    Kypridemos, C., et al. (2025). The IMPACTncd-Japan model: A comprehensive 
    microsimulation model for cardiovascular disease policy evaluation in Japan. 
    The Lancet Regional Health - Western Pacific, 54, 101623. 
    https://doi.org/10.1016/j.lanwpc.2025.101623

#### Citing the Software

When referring to the software implementation:

    Kypridemos, C., et al. (2025). IMPACTncd-Japan: R package for cardiovascular 
    disease microsimulation in Japan. R package version 0.0.1. 
    https://github.com/ChristK/IMPACTncd_Japan

### Contact for Collaborations

Researchers interested in collaborating or using the model for policy
analysis are encouraged to:

1.  **Read the documentation**: Start with the [Quick Start
    Guide](https://christk.github.io/IMPACTncd_Japan/articles/quickstart.md)
2.  **Review publications**: Understand the model’s capabilities and
    limitations
3.  **Contact the team**: Reach out via
    [GitHub](https://github.com/ChristK/IMPACTncd_Japan) or email

#### Research Opportunities

We welcome collaborations on:

- **Policy scenario analysis**: Evaluating specific health interventions
- **Model extensions**: Adding new diseases or risk factors
- **Validation studies**: Comparing with other models or real-world data
- **International comparisons**: Adapting the framework to other
  countries
